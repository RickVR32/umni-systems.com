Observer Codes
Four primary modes of awareness processing. Each person holds a ratio across them — determining how they perceive, filter, and act on information.
Balance Scale
A scored axis from −5 to +5 representing the degree of alignment between inner state and outer behaviour. The core output of every assessment.
Drift
The measurable gap between a person's baseline state and their current operating position. Drift accumulates silently — the tools are designed to surface it before it becomes visible in behaviour.
